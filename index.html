Miss NeoFemme

hello~ my name is tanu. i'm nineteen years old. I live in a college dorm with my friends and this is my little blog capturing my college life

some of my hobbies include getting ready and going out with friends, playing uno with them, going for to pet puppies on the street and in general just doing things that make me happy.

today marks about 1.5 yrs i have spent in my college dorm. in this time i have explored this world so much. i have come out of my shell, approached people in ways i was always too scared to, made beautiful friends and made a lifetime worth of memories. but unfortunatley i forget things at times. like how i met my friends on my frist year at campus. which i don't rememeber at all. so this is my attempt at capturing all the memories i have right now and storing them on this page forever.

i want to make this semester the happiest i have ever had till date. join me on this journey of making everyday a little bit more happening ;)
